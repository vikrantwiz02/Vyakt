About Gestura

Innovating solely for the purpose of bridging communication gaps.

Our Mission

Gestura is an innovative AI-powered Sign Language Translator designed to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and the Deaf/Hard-of-Hearing communities. Our mission is to make communication accessible, seamless, and inclusive for everyone.

The Problem

Millions of people worldwide use sign language as their primary means of communication. However, the majority of the hearing population does not understand sign language, creating significant barriers in education, healthcare, employment, and daily interactions.

Our Solution

Gestura leverages advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies to translate sign language gestures into text and speech in real-time. Conversely, it also translates spoken or written language into sign language animations, enabling two-way communication.

Key Features

Sign to Text

Real-time translation of hand gestures into text using your device's camera.

Text to Sign

Convert written text into animated sign language gestures for easy understanding.

Speech to Text

Voice recognition features to assist in converting spoken words into readable text.

Privacy Focused

Secure authentication and profile management to keep your user data safe.
